Micro-Learning: Dog-friendly Fruits and Vegetables

$uperSquad User Testing: Case Study

Gamified Financial Literacy Curriculum

I collaborated on a cross-functional team of production and instructional designers to create gamified interactives and videos to teach ages k through 6 financial literacy. Explore the $uperSquad world to get a taste for this open-sourced financial literacy curriculum. I also conducted a user testing case study with two participants (2nd and 4th grade females who read at grade level). In this testing, we focused on validating two improvements to the $uperSquad site: 1) the implementation of auto-play recordings to assist learners throughout the site and 2) the implementation of gifs to explain how to complete the different interactives throughout the Fitonia universe. You can access the user testing report case study here.

Technologies used include:

  • Google Suite
  • Microsoft Excel
