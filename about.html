About Rachel Dorsey, PhD

Just the highlights


Who I am: I am a learning experience designer, with a background in behavioral psycholinguistics, bilingualism, and inclusive pedagogy. My research background greatly informs my creative process, keeping learners at the center. I care that learners not only achieve the learning objectives, but that they experience moments of joy along the way!
